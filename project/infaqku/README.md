### ğŸ§® Zakat Calculator

Comprehensive calculator for all types of Zakat with accurate fiqh standards:

#### **1. Zakat Maal (Wealth)**

- Calculate Zakat on gold, silver, cash, and savings
- **Nisab**: 85 grams of gold or 595 grams of silver
- **Rate**: 2.5% of total wealth
- **Haul**: 1 lunar year
- Detailed information about calculation rules

#### **2. Zakat Fitr (Fitrah)**

- Calculate per-person Zakat Fitr
- Input number of people and price of rice per kg
- **Rate**: 2.5 kg of rice (or 3 liters of staple food) per person
- Due before Eid prayer

#### **3. Agricultural Zakat (Crops)**

- Calculate Zakat on harvest
- **Two irrigation systems**:
  - Rain/Natural: 10% (al-'ushr)
  - Manual/Paid irrigation: 5% (half al-'ushr)
- **Nisab**: 900 liters of produce
- Due at harvest time

#### **4. Business Zakat (Trade)**

- Calculate Zakat on business assets
- Subtract debts from total assets
- **Nisab**: Equivalent to 85 grams of gold
- **Rate**: 2.5% of net assets
- **Haul**: 1 lunar year

Each calculator includes:

- Clear input fields
- Instant results
- Info button with detailed fiqh rules
- Easy-to-understand explanations

#### **100% Offline**

#### **App Lock Protection**

- Secure your records with multiple authentication methods:

  - Encrypted backup file
- **Restore**: Import data from a backup file
  - Replaces current data with backup
  - Confirmation required before restore
- **Reset**: Delete all records permanently
  - Confirmation required
  - Cannot be undone

A: No. The app works completely offline.

A: Yes, 100% free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden costs.

A: All local data will be deleted. Make sure to backup your data first if you want to keep it.
