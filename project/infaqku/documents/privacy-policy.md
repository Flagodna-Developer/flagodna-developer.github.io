## Introduction

Thank you for choosing InfaqKu. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

InfaqKu is designed to respect your privacy completely. The app operates entirely on your device without any data collection, tracking, or analytics. All your Zakat and Infaq records remain 100% private and offline.

### What We Don't Collect:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Financial data or transaction details
- Zakat and Infaq records
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage statistics
- Analytics data
- Advertising identifiers
- Any other personal or non-personal information

---

## Data Storage

All data used by InfaqKu is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

- Zakat calculations and records
- Infaq management data
- Biometric authentication settings (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition, pattern)
- App preferences and settings
- Language preferences
- Display settings

**This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.**

## Third-Party Services

InfaqKu does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage services
- Payment processors
- Data synchronization services

## Permissions

InfaqKu requests the following permissions solely for app functionality:

### Internet Permission

- **Purpose:** Required by the Flutter framework for certain system functions
- **Usage:** The app operates completely offline; no data is transmitted over the internet
- **Data Access:** No data is collected or transmitted

### Network State Permission

- **Purpose:** To check network connectivity status
- **Usage:** Used only for local app functionality; no data is sent or received
- **Data Access:** No data is collected or transmitted

### Biometric Permission

- **Purpose:** To secure your worship records with fingerprint, face recognition, PIN, or pattern lock
- **Usage:** Provides app lock functionality to protect your private Zakat and Infaq data
- **Data Access:** Biometric data remains on your device and is managed by your device's secure system. We never access, store, or transmit your biometric information

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's core functionality and do not involve any data collection or transmission to external servers.
